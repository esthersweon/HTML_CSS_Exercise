Ad Mediation

Manage Multiple Demand Partners
Demand Partner Access

Select as many partners as you want from our portfolio of networks and demand partners. Exclude any partners you do not want to access your inventory. Through our self-service dashboard, optimize the performance of different networks and your direct-sold campaigns.

Simplify Your Tech Stack

Reduce the complexity of your tech stack and eliminate third party SDKs. Benefit from our highly advanced, distributed architecture. Get access to all major ad networks, including Google’s AdMob, Apple’s iAd, Facebook Audience Network, Twitter's MoPub, Millennial Media, and more.

Compatible with RTB and Programmatic

Access as many partners as needed to maximize fill. Prioritize the highest value traffic and easily add new partners for testing. Dynamic allocation technology based on business rules you create will ensure you monetize effectively.

Private RTB Marketplace

Real-Time Bidding Technology to Drive Revenue
Get Total Control

A private RTB marketplace gives publishers all the benefits of RTB's efficiency without losing control of advertisers. Set CPM floors and select the demand partners you want to work with while blocking others. Share more data with buyers you trust to increase advertising effectiveness and revenue.

Improve Monetization

Make buyers compete in real time to increase monetization by 5x or more. Work directly with agency trading desks to access premium demand. Reduce overhead by delivering those buys programmatically, instead of hiring salespeople.

Secure Data Platform

By only working with partners you trust, you can share first-party data to increase the value of your inventory. Increase your ROI by sharing demographic composition, behavioral patterns, geographic location, and in-app purchase history.

Ad Serving

A Powerful System for Precise Ad Delivery
Manage Direct-Sold Campaigns

Flight direct-sold campaigns using our self-service campaign tools. Ensure accurate campaign delivery by setting daily spend volumes, end user frequency capping, campaign start and end times, and other delivery options.

Real-Time Reporting and Analytics

Optimize campaigns and improve performance with our real-time system. Use robust analytics tools to send detailed reports to customers through an online dashboard or download the data and create custom reports in Excel.

Secure Data Platform

Experience little or no downtime with our robust and scalable ad delivery platform. The system operates with very low latency at a massive scale, serving billions of ads per month to some of the biggest publishers in mobile advertising.

Ad Formats

Innovative Formats to Engage Your Users
Native Ads

With native ads, sponsored content adapts to the publisher's user experience and fits seamlessly into the content stream. Get users to click on and share advertisers’ branded content instead of ignoring interruptive ad formats.

Video: Run High CPM Brand Video Campaigns

Increase monetization with high quality, low latency video campaigns. Get revenue from premium advertisers who want to extend the reach of movie promotions, television commercials, or even made-for-mobile video content.

Full Screen Interstitials: Stop Using Mobile Banner Ads

Interstitials offer engaging visual experiences at natural transition points in an app such as launch or a new game level. The simple close button in an interstitial allows users to easily navigate away, instead of trying to avoid clicking on interruptive banners.
