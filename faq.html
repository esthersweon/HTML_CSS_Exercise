10 Million Dollar RTB Fund for Mobile Publishers

  • What is TapSense?

    • TapSense is a full stack publisher monetization solution. Our platform offers ad mediation for all major networks, an ad server to flight direct campaigns or house ads, and RTB access to DSPs, agencies and brands. Read more about the platform here.

  • What is RTB?

    • RTB (real-time bidding) technology, allows publishers to sell their inventory in an automated fashion. It reduces operational overhead and increases competitiveness among buyers, providing higher CPMs.
